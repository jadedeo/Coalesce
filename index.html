About Us


Coalesce was created by four Girls Who Code to help you become your best activist. They wanted to make a site that made being an activist as easy and efficient as possible by uniting as many different resources together.


Charlotte

Alongside computer science, she thoroughly enjoys music, art, film, and photography.

She considers herself an avid activist and hopes to help inspire many others to join the cause through Coalesce!

Alaire

Her passions include theatre, music, and fashion.

She sometimes finds it difficult to even know where to start when it comes to activism, and hopes something like Coalesce can help others use their voice as an agent for change.

Shannen

She is looking forward to adopting multiple dogs and traveling the world.

She hopes to make a difference in her community by participating in various activist events and spreading awareness among her family and friends.

Jade

She enjoys reading and drawing, and particularly loves when the two intersect.

She is glad to have had a part in creating a site that serves as platform for uniting people for a good cause.
